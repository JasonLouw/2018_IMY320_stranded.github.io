About the game

Collect Resources

Collect things and build things and do things all in the name of progression!

Players can collect multiple resources in order to make progress. The player has to chop trees in order to collect wood which is used to construct most buildings. Stone is used for refining wood into planks and for upgrading existing buildings into their more powerful versions. Planks are a more refined version of wood. The player needs wood and stone to create planks. Planks are used in much the same way as stones in that they are used for upgrading buildings or for making more advanced buildings. Coconuts are used for crafting weapons such as the catapults and upgrading buildings.

Fight off enemies

You know those terrible creatures that are trying to kill you? Kill them first!

To fight off enemies the player must strategically set up defences in their base so as to prevent enemies from destroying helpful buildings like the storage hut or the more vital building: The Hut, as without this hut the player is overrun and loses the game. The player has the chance to take base defences into their own hands during an attack as they can man the turret on top of the hut to shoot at oncoming enemies.

Sail to safety

Forget horses and cars. You're leaving on a boat!

After having survived numerous attacks and having built the ship (which will take up an entire level), the player will swiftly leave the island and return to civilization.
